The Camino Within is donating the proceeds of
the book to UNHCR | Canada


Helping refugees is a cause I care about deeply. This is why I chose to do a bike trip around Europe and raise some money and awareness for the UNHCR refugee cause. I also made it a point to give a monthly contribution on behalf of my company (Cap.TaiM Marketing Inc.). I strongly believe in supporting and helping refugees, I know it is not easy for them. As I was born in Lebanon, I do understand what it is like to struggle for safety and survival. As I live in Canada now, I wake up every day grateful for where I am. Sadly, this is not the case for more than 67 million refugees worldwide. They have every right to live a safe and dignified life as much as we do! By supporting UNHCR with this meaningful cause, you will help us meet our goal to provide life-saving assistance to refugees all over the world. This summer I will be launching my first book, “The Camino Within,” and my goal for this book is to truly support others whether through empowerment or through donations, I will be providing the proceeds of the first 200 books sold to the UNHCR. UNHCR provides shelter, food, water, medical care and other survival aid to those who were forced to flee their homes to escape violence or persecution. By donating to my page, you are helping UNHCR reach even more people. Every bit counts! The process is fast, easy, and secure. Your contribution will make a huge difference in the lives of refugees. Not to mention that your donation is 100% tax deductible.
Thank you so much for being a helping hand!
